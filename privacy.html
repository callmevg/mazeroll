Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data and respect your privacy.

Last Updated: July 12, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Maze Roll is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Device Motion Data: We access your device's accelerometer and gyroscope sensors to enable tilt-based gameplay. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Game Progress: Your game progress, including level completion status and streak records, is stored locally on your device.
  • App Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience, but this data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide core game functionality (motion-controlled gameplay)
  • To save your game progress and achievements
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To enhance user experience through anonymous analytics

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy and data security are important to us:

  • All game data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS/iPadOS storage mechanisms
  • Motion sensor data is processed in real-time and is not stored or transmitted
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information
  • Your data remains on your device and is not shared with third parties

4. Third-Party Services

Maze Roll may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple Game Center: If you choose to use Game Center, your achievements and leaderboard data will be handled according to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • iCloud: If enabled, your game progress may be synchronized across your devices using iCloud, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

Maze Roll is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not contain:

  • Social features or communication capabilities
  • In-app purchases
  • Advertising or marketing content
  • Links to external websites or services

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties. Your information remains private and is only used as described in this policy.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • You can disable motion sensors through iOS Settings (though this will affect gameplay)
  • You can opt out of analytics through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@mazeroll.app
