About Maze Roll

Maze Roll is a captivating puzzle game that combines traditional maze navigation with modern motion-controlled gameplay. Built with Apple's SpriteKit framework, the game delivers smooth performance and engaging physics-based interactions.

The game features procedurally generated mazes and hand-crafted levels that challenge players to think strategically while maintaining precise control over the ball's movement. Each level presents unique obstacles and pathways that require careful navigation and timing.

Whether you're looking for a quick gaming session or an extended puzzle-solving experience, Maze Roll adapts to your play style with its progressive difficulty system and engaging streak mechanics.

Support & FAQ

How do I control the ball?
Simply tilt your device in the direction you want the ball to move. The game uses your device's built-in motion sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope) to detect tilting movements and translate them into ball movement within the maze.
What are streaks and how do they work?
Streaks track how many levels you complete consecutively without failing. Each successful level completion adds to your current streak. Your highest streak is saved and displayed as a personal best to beat.
Can I play without motion controls?
The game is specifically designed around motion controls for an immersive experience. Motion sensors (accelerometer and gyroscope) are required to play the game effectively.
Is the game compatible with my device?
Maze Roll requires iOS devices with accelerometer and gyroscope sensors. The game supports both portrait and landscape orientations and is optimized for iPhone and iPad.
How do I report bugs or request features?
You can contact us through the support email listed below. We appreciate feedback and actively work to improve the game based on player suggestions.
